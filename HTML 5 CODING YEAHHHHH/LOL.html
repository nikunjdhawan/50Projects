League OF Legends

League of Legends is a 2009 multiplayer online battle arena video game
developed and published by Riot Games for Microsoft Windows and
macOS. Inspired by a modified version of Warcraft III called
Defense of the Ancients, Riot's founders sought to develop a stand-alone game in the same genre.
